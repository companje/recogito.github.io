Customizing Annotation Styles

Need a bit more flexibility with the visual appearance of your annotations than Annotorious provides out of the box? Customizing styles is easy! Depending on what you need, you have two options:

  1. Change the styles globally, by overriding or replacing the built-in CSS stylesheet
  2. Write your own JavaScript Formatter function to apply styles based on specific rules
